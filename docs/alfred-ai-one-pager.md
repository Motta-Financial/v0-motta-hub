## What is ALFRED Ai?

ALFRED Ai is Motta's internal AI operating system — a unified workspace that connects every system the firm relies on (Karbon, Calendly, Zoom, Stripe, Jotform, Resend, Supabase, and more) and layers a Claude-powered assistant on top.

Where other firms rely on disconnected tools and manual handoffs, ALFRED Ai gives every team member a single interface to query client data, draft communications, manage engagements, and execute workflows in natural language. It is the engine that lets a lean team operate with the throughput of a much larger firm.

---

## Core Capabilities

**Client Intelligence**
ALFRED Ai maintains a real-time, queryable view of every client relationship — engagements, work items, invoices, communications, and meeting history — synchronized from Karbon and the firm's project management systems. CPAs ask questions in plain English ("which clients have overdue 1040s?") and get an answer in seconds.

**Lead Intake & Research**
New prospects submitting the firm's intake form are automatically enriched with web research, scored, and routed to the right partner. ALFRED Ai drafts a research-backed response to the prospect's specific tax or accounting question before the partner ever sees the lead — turning hours of pre-call prep into a one-click review.

**Engagement Workflow**
From proposal to engagement letter to invoice to project kickoff, ALFRED Ai orchestrates the full client onboarding sequence. Calendly bookings auto-generate Zoom meetings, Stripe payment links, and Karbon work items without anyone touching a spreadsheet.

**Internal Operations**
A daily morning briefing emails each team member their priorities, upcoming meetings, and outstanding items in a concise, conversational format. A weekly "Tommy Awards" recap recognizes team contributions. Triage queues, training modules, and feedback loops are all surfaced inside the same platform.

**Compliance & Audit Trail**
Every AI interaction, model decision, and workflow execution is logged with full attribution. Partners can audit any AI-drafted response, see which model was used, and override prompts on a per-use-case basis from the admin console.

## Technology Foundation

ALFRED Ai runs on a modern, enterprise-grade stack:

- **AI Models** — Anthropic Claude (Sonnet for reasoning + tool use, Haiku for high-volume tasks), routed through the Vercel AI Gateway
- **Infrastructure** — Vercel serverless platform with SOC 2-compliant Supabase Postgres
- **Integrations** — Karbon, Calendly, Zoom, Stripe, Jotform, Resend, Vercel Blob
- **Security** — Row-level security on all client data, encrypted at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, complete audit logging
